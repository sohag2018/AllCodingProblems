What are the steps of getting JDBC 
1.Loading the Driver: a.  Class.forName()  OR  b.  DriverManager.registerDriver()
2.Create the connection:     Connection con=DriverManager.getConnection(url,user, pass)
3.Create a statement: Statement st=con.createStatement()
4.Execute the query: con.executeQuery()
5.close the connection: con.close()
